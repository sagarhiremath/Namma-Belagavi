Belagavi central Bus stand:-


Chief Minister Basavaraj Bommai on Tuesday inaugurated a Hi-Tech Central Bus Stand (CBS) at Belagavi, built at a cost of over Rs 32.41 crores.
The modern bus station has been built on an area of 3.08 acres with 41 bus bays, bus circulation and parking areas, auto-rickshaw lane, passenger entry/ exit, Terminal building consisting of passenger circulation and waiting areas, ticket/ reservation counters, cloak room, first-aid room, waiting rooms, passenger amenities like drinking water, toilets, yatri nivas etc., restaurant, shops, kiosks and administrative offices, crew restrooms, parcel office and other facilities. The adjacent NWKRTC Divisional Workshop which is is located on 4.68 acres will be taken up in the next phase.



Belagavi Railway station:-

Belagavi Railway station is in Belagavi district making it an important railway station in the Indian state of Karnataka. The station code name of Belagavi is ‘BGM’. As part of one of the busiest and populated Indian states, Karnataka, the Belagavi railway station is known amongst the top hundred train ticket booking and train traveling stations of the Indian Railway. The total number of trains that pass through Belagavi (BGM) junction is 53.




Belagavi Airport:-

About:-


1) It is established by Royal Air Force in 1942. Indian Airlines commenced operations from 1947.

2) From the year 1989 to 2011, various private Airlines operated at Belagavi Airport.

3) From the year 2012 M/s. Spicejet started their operation and continued till date. Upto May-2018, M/s. Spicejet operated to 4 major cities i.e. Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bengaluru     & Chennai.

4) UDAN-3 changed the history of Belagavi Airport. 13 city connections awarded to 5 major Airlines under UDAN-3.

5) From 1st May-2019 to till date, there is 13 cities direct flights (Bengaluru, Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Kadappa, Mysuru, Indore, Ahmedabad, Surat, Tirupati, Jodhpur,     Nasik & Surat) by 5 Airlines i.e. M/s. Alliance Air, Spicejet, Star Air, Indigo & Trujet and Jaipur & Nagpur yet to be connected by M/s. Star Air Airline.

6) Night parking permission granted for two (02) no’s of Aircrafts EMB-145 of M/s. Star Air (Ghodawat Group).
